Invest4Edu:

We help build Foundation for your kid's bright future

  • September 26, 2021

Education is the best gift that any parent would like to give to their children. Whether belonging to a low income group or from middle-class background, each parent's most cherished dream is to be able to provide their child with top quality education, thereby securing their child's future. One can still manage basic education without much hassle but when it comes to enrolling their child to degree courses such as Engineering, Medical or Management, it becomes difficult for many parents to cope up with the demand of the fee structure and living expenditure it involves. And more so, it turns out to be a backbreaker for parents to shell out such a big amount, if their child wants to pursue his/her studies abroad. No parent would like to compromise on their child's dream education and consequently the job for the lack of adequate funds. This needs strategic planning from parents to avoid any shortage of money to fund their child's higher education. Here comes the role of invest4edu, a platform that aims to cater to all the requirements one needs for pursuing graduation/post graduation in India or even abroad. Invest4edu offers parents well structured, tailor made, personalized investment plans according to one's income and need, to save for their children's education when they grow up. It will help you to estimate the cost of the course you are looking for (course fee, books etc), the living cost (accomodation, food etc), even the travelling expenses and insurances required (if going abroad for further studies) through their specialized software program which calculates all the expenses with the help of special algorithm that runs on the data collected from all over the world by their research team.

Invest4edu vision is not just limited to providing funding solution to their clients but making the whole process of career choice, decision making easier and accessible to all groups of people whether in tier 1, tier 2 or tier 3 cities of our country with the help of their expertise through a well learned and research oriented team of professionals.

Unlike any other fintech Company, the USP of invest4edu is, it is first of its kind platform which is solely dedicated to solve all the problems and provide the solution in the best possible way related to education and higher studies. It has a unique blend of global information about higher education and education savings financial products, helping to attain your child's educational goals in the easiest and most uncomplicated way.

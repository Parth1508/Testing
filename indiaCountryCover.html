  • Cost of living is the last thing to worry about in India. It is way lower compared to giant education hubs such as the US, UK and Australia.

Student friendly cities

Finding a perfect student friendly city in a foriegn country is another one of the top reasons to study in India. If students decide to take the leap and study abroad in India, they are likely to study in one of the flourished cities in the country.

Each city has its unique traditions, cultures and values to take back home. Yet, there are few cities that shine among the rest as lucrative study abroad destinations.

Thrive in Multicultural society
